# Mula Platform — North Star

## Vision
Mula Group is the technology ecosystem that transforms how businesses operate in the digital age.

## Mission
We design, build and deploy integrated technology solutions — from AI agents to e-commerce platforms to cybersecurity — enabling companies to focus on growth while we handle the technology.

## Core Principles

### 1. Ecosystem over Silos
Every service, every app, every component works together. No standalone islands. The whole is greater than the sum of its parts.

### 2. Technology First, Not Technology Only
We lead with technology but communicate in business outcomes. Clients don't need to understand RAG — they need to understand "faster lead qualification".

### 3. Execution Over Presentations
We build and ship. Every sprint produces working code. Documentation follows implementation, not precedes it.

### 4. Premium Quality Always
"Minimum viable" does not mean "low quality". Every component, every page, every interaction feels enterprise-grade.

### 5. Polish Market, Global Standards
We serve Polish businesses with world-class technology. Our stack, our design, our engineering matches Silicon Valley standards.

## Product KPIs
- **Landing page conversion rate**: >3% (contact form submissions)
- **Page speed**: Lighthouse >90 on all metrics
- **Domain health**: 100% SSL coverage, 0 broken links
- **Code quality**: 0 TypeScript errors, 0 ESLint errors
- **Deploy frequency**: Every sprint deploys to production

## Technology North Star
- Monorepo with shared design system
- All apps use same component library
- Auto-deploy on push to master
- Zero manual server management (fully Vercel)
- Real-time analytics on all properties

## What We Don't Do
- We don't build standalone, disconnected projects
- We don't use WordPress unless explicitly justified
- We don't ship without documentation
- We don't skip code review
- We don't compromise on performance
